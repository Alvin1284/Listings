About this property

SUMMARY

Welcome to your serene and stylish 1-bedroom retreat in the heart of Westlands near Sarit Centre. Thoughtfully designed for ultimate comfort, this exquisite space blends modern finishes with a homely touch — perfect for both short and long stays. Enjoy unbeatable location, fast Wi-Fi, and warm Kenyan hospitality. Karibu!

LOCATION & NEARBY

We are located in the residential neighborhood of Westlands with excellent access to shopping and entertainment:

  • • 5 minutes walk to Sarit Centre
  • • 5 minutes drive to Westgate Mall
  • • 5 minutes walking distance to GTC Mall
  • • 15 minutes drive to Kuona Artist Collective for visual Arts

For further suggestions on where to dine and explore, please find our elaborative Nairobi Guidebook in your unit.

TRANSPORT

The apartment building is located about 20 km from Nairobi Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. It takes around 28 minutes to get from the airport to the apartment via the new Expressway.
