About this property

SUMMARY

Experience urban boho styled luxury living in this expansive two-bedroom apartment, where contemporary design meets unparalleled comfort. We offer a blend of comfort, style, and convenience for your stay.

Located in a central city locale, you'll have easy access to the city's top attractions, dining hot spots, and entertainment venues. Whether you're here for business or leisure, this upscale retreat provides the perfect home base for your unforgettable stay in Nairobi.

GUEST ACCESS

Guests have use of the entire apartment and can also access the property's Swimming pool, gym, and the kid's play area.

TRANSPORT

The apartment building is located about 22 km from Nairobi (Jomo Kenyatta) International Airport. It takes around 35 minutes to get from the airport to the apartment via the new Expressway.
